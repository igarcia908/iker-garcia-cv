About

I am a Senior AI Research Engineer and Data Science Lead with over six years of experience building intelligent systems for real-world industrial problems. Currently at Hubbell, I lead the Data Science effort in the Bilbao team, acting as the primary bridge between AI research and production software systems — coordinating closely with software engineering teams in the United States to turn research prototypes into production-ready solutions.

My work spans the full arc from exploratory research to deployed ML pipelines: machine learning, combinatorial optimisation, time-series analysis, smart-grid intelligence, and large-scale data engineering. I am equally comfortable presenting novel algorithms in peer-reviewed journals and shipping code that runs in production.

I actively embrace modern AI tooling and agentic workflows — including AI coding assistants like Claude Code — to accelerate development, automate repetitive tasks, and prototype AI-powered applications rapidly. I believe the next wave of productivity in software engineering will be driven by developers who can collaborate effectively with AI systems.

Alongside my industry work, I am pursuing a PhD in Computer Engineering (major: Artificial Intelligence) at the University of the Basque Country (UPV/EHU), with publications in top-tier journals including Elsevier and IEEE.
